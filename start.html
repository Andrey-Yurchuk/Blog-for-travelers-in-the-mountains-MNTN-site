Safety in the mountains

Let us know about your plans

The first thing we would like you to understand is that the most important thing in the mountains is your safety. One of the most common mistakes that tourists make is not telling others about their plans. If you are going to hike in the mountains, be sure to inform your relatives (family, friends), the staff at the hotel reception or the owners of the accommodation where you are staying, as well as mountain rescuers. Situations during tours can be very unpredictable – you can get into adverse weather conditions, despite the previously good weather, just get lost in the woods or become a victim of an accident. It is necessary that in case of your prolonged absence or lack of contact with you, close people know that they need to look for you. Here you can read about our recommendations and what to wear for a walk in the mountains.

Knowledge of the area

Novice tourists who do not know the specifics of mountains and specific mountain routes, as well as those who cannot properly plan a mountain trip, are often exposed to unpleasant situations. The most common mistake is ignorance of the rules of movement in the mountains, as well as the basic elements of trip planning. Tourists do not check the information on the map, in the tourist book or in the guidebook. Tourist guides and maps help you decide where to go on a trip, and also provide a lot of information, including about the difficulties that may arise in the area.
